• On the behaviour of $\gamma\log p$ modulo~1 .
    Soumis \`a Annals of Math. 36pp, (2011) De quoi cela parle : We prove non-trivial lower bounds for sums of type ∑p∼ Pg(γlogp), where g is a non-negative 2π-periodical function and γ is a given parameter. As an application we prove that ζ(1+it)±1≪loglog(9+|t|) and extend the zero-free region of the Riemann zeta-function.
